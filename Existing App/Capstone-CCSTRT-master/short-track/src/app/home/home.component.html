2019 Season Details

Info: Register online. Check in at 5:45-6:15pm each race night, then groups start by race category at 6:30pm, the youngest group goes for 10 minutes, juniors 20 minutes, adults 30 minutes. Get in as many laps as you can.

Price: $15 plus $5 for plate fee if you need one.

Spring: 6 nights, every Tuesday starting May 7th.

Fall: 4 nights, every Tuesday starting Sept 10th.

Where: Top of Young St. in Truro at the old dump entrance (roughly 745 Young St). Follow signs and the crowd.
